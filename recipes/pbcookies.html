peanut butter cookies recipe

home

description

3-ingredient peanut butter cookies

ingredients

  • 1 cup peanut butter
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 1 large egg

steps

  1. preheat the oven to 350 degrees.
  2. mix peanut butter, sugar and egg together in a bowl using an electric mixer until smooth and creamy.
  3. roll mixture into balls on ungreased baking sheet and flatten with a fork. bake in preheated oven for about 10 minutes.
