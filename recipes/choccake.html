flourless chocolate cake recipe

home

description

decadently rich and delicious gluten-free flourless chocolate cake, easy to make in under 1 hour!

ingredients

  • 8 large eggs, cold
  • 1 pound dark chocolate, coarsely chopped
  • 1 cup salted butter, cut into 16 pieces
  • optional toppings: powdered sugar and berries

steps

  1. in a stand mixer, beat eggs at high speed until the volume doubles.
  2. melt the chocolate and butter and fold in the beaten egg foam.
  3. scrape the batter into the lined springform pan and bake for 35-40 minutes.
