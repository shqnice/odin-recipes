bread recipe

home

description

crusty white bread - takes a lot longer to prepare but it might be the best loaf of artisan bread you've baked at home

ingredients

  • 500g strong white bread flour
  • 4g fast-action dried yeast
  • 10g fine sea salt

steps

  1. tip the flour into a large bowl along with the yeast and pour over 325g of room temperature water. mix until a dough forms. cover with a damp tea towel and leave to rest for 30 minutes.
  2. use the stretch and fold method and leave to prove in the fridge overnight until doubled in size.
  3. tip it out onto a work surface and knock the air out. form the dough ball into a tight, rounded shape and leave to prove in a warm place for 1 hour.
  4. preheat the oven to 230c. lower the dough on parchment paper into the hot casserole and bake for 25 minutes. remove the lid and bake for 5-10 minutes.
  5. cool on a wire rack for 40 minutes before slicing.
